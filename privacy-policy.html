Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 13, 2025

Last Updated: August 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pocket Oracle ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how Ivikos collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application Pocket Oracle ("App"). Our App provides AI-powered I Ching divination and guidance services. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Account information when you sign in with Google or email (name, email address, profile picture)
  • Authentication credentials managed through Firebase Authentication
  • User preferences and app settings
  • Communication preferences and contact information

I Ching Reading Data:

  • Questions you submit for I Ching consultations
  • Reading results and interpretations generated by our AI system
  • Reading history and summaries stored securely in Firebase Firestore
  • Timestamps and reading metadata

Usage Information:

  • App interactions and usage patterns
  • Chat conversations with the AI assistant
  • Device information (device type, operating system, app version)
  • Log information (IP address, access times, feature usage)

3. Third-Party Services

OpenAI Integration:

Our App uses OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model to provide AI-powered I Ching interpretations and guidance. When you submit questions or interact with our AI assistant, your queries are processed through OpenAI's API. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Terms of Use to understand how OpenAI handles data.

Google Sign-In:

When you choose to sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) as permitted by your Google account settings. This integration is governed by Google's privacy policies.

Firebase Services:

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. Your reading summaries and user data are stored securely in Firebase Firestore, protected by Firebase Authentication using your unique user ID. Only you can access your personal reading data.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized I Ching readings and AI-powered guidance
  • Maintain your reading history and summaries securely
  • Authenticate your account and ensure data security
  • Improve our AI responses and interpretation accuracy
  • Contact you regarding app updates, security issues, or service improvements
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Detect, investigate and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized access

5. Data Security and Storage

Firebase Security:

Your personal reading data is stored in Firebase Firestore with robust security measures:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access is restricted using Firebase Authentication with your unique user ID
  • Only authenticated users can access their own data
  • We implement Firebase Security Rules to prevent unauthorized access

However, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following limited situations:

  • With Your Consent: We may disclose your personal information for any purpose with your explicit consent
  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that perform services on our behalf (OpenAI for AI processing, Google for authentication and storage)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of assets, or acquisition of our business

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or reading data to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Account information: Until you delete your account
  • Reading history and summaries: Until you delete them or your account
  • Usage data: Up to 2 years for analytics and improvement purposes
  • Legal compliance data: As required by applicable law

You can delete your reading history, individual readings, or your entire account at any time through the app settings.

8. Your Rights and Controls

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View and download your personal information and reading history
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Delete your readings, reading history, or entire account
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Data Portability: Export your reading data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below or use the controls available in the app.

9. Contact Information Use

Your contact information (email address) will only be used for:

  • Account-related communications (password resets, security alerts)
  • Important app updates and service announcements
  • Customer support and technical assistance
  • Security notifications and fraud prevention
  • Periodic app improvement surveys (with your consent)

We will never use your contact information for unsolicited marketing or share it with third parties for their marketing purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers (OpenAI, Google Firebase) operate. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification through the app or email

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
